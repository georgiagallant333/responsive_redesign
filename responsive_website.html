Bird Camp

Dedicated to the promotion of pet bird needs & enlightenment of their owners.

Services

We primarily offer boarding and grooming. We also sell food and other bird supplies upon request. If interested in food or supplies email us at orders@birdcamp.com!

Boarding

Each bird (or your multiple birds) will be kept in its own cage. Water and food are provided twice daily. Fresh fruit and vegetables are offered daily at no extra charge.

Grooming

We groom nails and wings for all birds. Beaks are groomed only when corrective grooming is necessary.

What To Bring

  1. A current health certificate within the last 12 month Birdcamp is not responsible for sickness or death unless such an incidence is the result of gross negligence.

  2. Please come with a towel and bring your bird in a carrier. It is not safe to bring your bird on your shoulder or in your hand.

  3. Any medication or special food needed.

Boarding Rates for Unusual Circumstances

  1. Multiple Birds: There will be an additional 15% charge for multiple birds in the same cage
  2. Birds Staying > 10 days: require a 50% deposit
  3. Birds Requiring Extra Care: There will be a nominal charge for extra care (administering medication, preparing food for special diets, etc.)
